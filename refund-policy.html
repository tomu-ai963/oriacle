✦ Refund Policy

Effective: June 2026

This policy explains when refunds are available for Oriacle subscriptions.

1. Subscription Plans

  • Free — No payment required. No refund applicable.
  • Oriacle Plus — $9.99 / month
  • Oriacle Pro — $19.99 / month

2. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain full access until then. We do not charge cancellation fees.

3. Refunds

Subscription charges are generally non-refundable for any partially used billing period. If you cancel after being charged for a new billing cycle, access continues through that period but a refund will not be issued.

4. Exceptions

We will issue a full refund in the following circumstances:

  • You were charged due to a technical error on our part
  • You contact us within 48 hours of your first-ever subscription charge and have not used any paid features

To request an exception refund, email us at tomu.ai.dev@gmail.com with your account email and the charge date. We aim to respond within 3 business days.

5. Free Trial

Oriacle does not currently offer a free trial that requires a credit card. The Free plan is available indefinitely at no charge. If a trial period with billing is introduced in future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Payment Disputes

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly, please contact us before initiating a chargeback. Unresolved chargebacks may result in account suspension.

7. Contact

Refund requests and billing questions: tomu.ai.dev@gmail.com
